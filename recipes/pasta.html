Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 6 strips bacon
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Reserve 1 cup of the pasta water.
  2. In a large skillet, cook bacon until crispy. Remove from heat and chop into small pieces.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan cheese, black pepper, and salt.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the egg mixture, tossing to coat. If the pasta is too dry, add some of the reserved pasta water to moisten.
  5. Stir in the chopped bacon.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
